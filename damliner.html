Dam Liner

At Irri-Hub Ke, we acknowledge the urgent need for rainwater harvesting due to climate change disruptions. With prolonged dry seasons and unpredictable floods becoming the norm, adaptation is crucial.

We offer UV-protected HDPE liners, highly durable and resistant to tear, puncture, and stress crack, ensuring long-term use. Recommended thickness varies based on pan size and interior smoothness, providing a comprehensive solution for effective rainwater harvesting and storage in line with sustainable water management practices.
